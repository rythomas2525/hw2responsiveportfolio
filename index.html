About Me


Hi, I’m Ryan Thomas. Here is a little bit about me. I started a vape juice company when I was 19, and ran it successfully for seven years. However, recently I’ve decided to close the doors. I currently work for a company that produces men’s rings; Lashbrook Designs. I operate various CNC machines and I really enjoy it.

I was born and raised in Kamas, Utah on a cattle ranch. It was a small farming-outdoorsy town when I was a child. Growing up there gave me a strong love for animals, being in nature, and enjoying the outdoors.

I spend a lot of my free time researching and learning about my passions and new things I’m interested in. Mushroom hunting, gardening, and beekeeping are a few of my favorite hobbies. Along with kayaking, camping, and hanging out with my huskies named Akilla
and June.
